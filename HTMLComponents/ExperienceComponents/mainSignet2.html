Signet Jewelers 2024
A immersive twelve-week cooperative experience as a Software Development intern. May 2024 - Aug. 2024

I started this project in the Summer of 2022. I was at a standpoint at my love for Computer Science and school had just let out. With me being bored and interviewing for a Summer job in my hometown, I decided I need a website to keep track of all of my professional achievements / acolades and thus, this website was born.

By absolutely no means is this project nearly where I want to be in terms of development, but by creating this webpage, I dramatically increased my knowledge in HTML, CSS, and Javascript. Re-learning these languages I used 4+ years ago in highschool was incredibly fun. Even though this project is always a work in progress, I'm super happy with the outcome. I'll constantly be updating & changing things on this webpage, & on the left is pictures concerning the process of creating this project.
