GymThis
A comprehensive app facilitating gym-goers in tracking workouts, calories, and more. Dec. 2023 - Present

This is a side project web app that my friend and fellow classmate, Jahnzeb Bastaki, and I are in the early stages of creating. We plan for this project to be done by the start of summer 2024.

Do you ever feel like there are too many things to remember in the gym? How many reps or sets, what each exercise does, and the progress you need to be making have all been integrated into GymThis. GymThis allows new and experienced gymgoers alike to help store important information regarding the gym.
