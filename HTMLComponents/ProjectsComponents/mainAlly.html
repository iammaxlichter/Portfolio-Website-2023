Ally's Photography
A beautiful photography website built in HTML, CSS Bootstrap, and JavaScript. Feb. 2024 - Mar. 2024

Made for a close friend, Ally Judge, this website was specifically designed and curated for her photography business. It comes complete a beautiful array of all of her most prized photos along with a little section about who she is, and a section where you can directly contact her about booking a session with her.

Ally has big dreams ahead of her and photography is something that she truly enjoys doing.
