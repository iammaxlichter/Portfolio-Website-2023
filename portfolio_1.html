Portfolio Website 2022
A foundational HTML & CSS digital portfolio detailing my journey in computer science. May 2022 - Dec. 2022

I started this project in the Summer of 2022. I was at a standstill at my love for Computer Science and school had just let out. With me being bored and interviewing for a Summer job in my hometown, I decided I needed a website to keep track of all of my professional achievements / acolades and thus, I created my own portfolio website.

By absolutely no means was this project nearly where I want to be in terms of development, but by creating the webpage, I dramatically increased my knowledge in HTML, CSS, and Javascript. Re-learning these languages I used years ago in highschool was incredibly fun. Even though the webpage was a work in progress, I'm super happy with the outcome. Until the iteration of the website you are seeing this description, I was constantly updating & changing things on the webpage, At the bottom of the page, are pictures of the this first iteration of my portfolio website
