Karoshi
A crazy platformer game with a twist! August 2023 - December 2023

During my sixth semester in my undergraduate degree at UT Dallas, I enrolled in the course known as "Software Engineering." Over the course of the semester, I, along with 4 other teammates would develop the interactive and cohesive game known as Karoshi.


Karoshi was an extremely intuitive side project that I had honor to be a part of. The game has two levels, with the same objective; to reach the end. During both levels you will encounter different obstactles that you have to overcome. Below, you can find a download link to Karoshi to play it yourself, along with the GitHub associated with the project.
